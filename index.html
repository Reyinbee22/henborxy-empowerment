About HENBORYX Empowerment - 2×2 Matrix

HENBORYX Empowerment uses a revolutionary 2×2 matrix system where everyone helps everyone succeed.


How The 2×2 Matrix Works:
  • You register and bring 2 people directly
  • Those 2 people each bring 2 people = 4 more
  • Your Total Network = 6 people
  • At 6 people, you leave Starter and enter Stage 1
  • The Pattern Repeats: Those 6 people each do the same!
Stage Rewards:
  • Starter: ₦3,000 (Full amount withdrawable)
  • Stage 1: ₦25,000 (₦7,500 withdrawable, 70% food reward)
  • Stage 2: ₦50,000 (₦15,000 withdrawable, 70% food reward)
  • Stage 3: ₦100,000 (₦30,000 withdrawable, 70% food reward)
  • Stage 4: ₦250,000 (₦75,000 withdrawable, 70% food reward)
  • Stage 5: ₦500,000 (₦150,000 withdrawable, 70% food reward)
Key Rule: To advance to the next stage, ALL your direct referrals must have completed their own 2×2 matrix first!
